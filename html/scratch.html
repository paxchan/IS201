Avatar The Last Airbender

Why Avatar the Last Airbender is the greatest series of all time

  1. Plot
    • Best universe creation
    • Powerful story and morals
    • Keeps you on the edge of your seat
  2. Characters
    • Zuko has the best character arc of all time
    • Characters are able to be humorous and serious at the correct times
    • The relationships between characters show genuine friendship and loyalty
  3. Lessons
    • Many parallels to religion and faith
    • Teaches us to respect each other and our differences
    • Shows the power of forgiveness and change
